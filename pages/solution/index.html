Powering Edge Intelligence with High-speed Interconnects

Qualitas Semiconductor’s interconnect IP solutions for Mobile and Embedded Edge AI systems enable seamless, power-efficient data transfer in compact,
high-density environments. Supporting AI-driven edge applications such as smart sensors, vision modules, wearables, and edge processors, our IP helps
customers meet the challenges of low-latency, high-bandwidth communication under stringent power and area constraints.

Powering Next-gen Automotive Systems
with Reliable Interconnect IP

Qualitas Semiconductor’s interconnect IP solutions for automotive systems are designed to meet the growing demands of data-intensive, safety-critical
applications. From ADAS and in-vehicle infotainment to centralized compute platforms, our IP delivers high-throughput, low-latency communication with
automotive-grade reliability. The IP is developed through an FSM-based functional safety process and is on track for ASIL-D certification, with additional plans
to complete both ASIL-B and ASIL-D certification for future platforms.

1) 보고사항 : 감사보고, 영업보고, 외부감사인 선임보고,내부회계관리제도 운영실태보고
2) 부의안건

나. 제2호 의안 : 정관 일부 변경의 건
다. 제3호 의안 : 사내이사 선임의 건 
제3-1호 의안 : 김두호 사내이사 선임 (중임)
제3-2호 의안 : 최광천 사내이사 선임 (중임)
라. 제4호 의안 : 이사 보수한도 승인의 건 
마. 제5호 의안 : 감사 보수한도 승인의 건 
바. 제6호 의안 : 임원 퇴직금 규정 변경의 건
