Q-CARES

Qualitas Semiconductor assists successful completion of your SoC project through our high‑quality and high‑speed interconnect IPs and professional tech‑support based on our customer oriented‑mindset.

  • Technical support provided by dedicated AEs with extensive experience and expertise

  • Swift responses covering the entire steps of your project, based on a well‑structured system

  • Devoted service to satisfy the needs of individual customers and thorough follow‑up management

Project Life Cycle

  • IP product consultation
  • Development of IP
  • Supporting IP integration
  • Support Silicon Test & Evaluation

IP product consultation

IP product consultation involves IP consulting and providing initial documentation and materials.

Development of IP

Development of IP includes project kick‑off training, DK quality assurance, provision of customized DK, and an up‑to‑date Process Design Kit (PDK).

Supporting IP integration

Includes design reviews based on the customer’s SoC, on‑site checklist, dedicated support for complex challenges like S/I analysis and protocol verification, and system‑level IP verification with simulation models and test plans.

Support Silicon Test & Evaluation

A troubleshooting guide with relevant software is provided, along with access to an in‑house test lab for IP validation.
